Go Ahead (以家人之名)

Aster Signal: 8.9 ★

Narrative: Three friends from troubled families form a unique, lasting bond — but their pasts soon cast a cloud over their lives. Growing up in dysfunctional households, three individuals who are unrelated by blood treat each other like family as they yearn for the love that they cannot find at home.

Cast Narrative: Song Weilong portrays Ling Xiao, the eldest "brother" who carries the weight of a tragic past and the burden of caregiving stillness.
